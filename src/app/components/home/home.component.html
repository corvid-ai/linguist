Welcome to Linguist

A Corvid-AI Research Project

Linguist is a data curation tool built to collect and carefully annotate large textual data on local african languages.

Currently, only 13 out of the estimated 1500 local African languages in Africa are supported by Google translate. This is mainly due to the lack of reliable, undocumented and insufficient annotated datasets on local African languages. However, our preliminary research findings indicate that there is a huge amount of textual data dispersed and floating across the web. Someone needs to do the heavy lifting of bringing all these datasets to a common location. This is where Project Nightingale comes in. Project Nightingale is an open source project aimed at providing a platform for gathering, documenting and annotating textual data on local African languages. Night seeks to leverage ML and NLP techniques to develop API’s, tools, packages, and applications. At Corvid.AI we believe in the democratization of AI to make tools, datasets, API’s accessible to everyone.

Linguist is the data gathering phase of Project Nightingale, an open source project aimed at building cutting-edge API’s that will make textual data on Local Afrian Languages more accessible to researchers, developers and the general public. You can help this cause by simply translating a word, a sentence or phrase. Remember every word counts.
