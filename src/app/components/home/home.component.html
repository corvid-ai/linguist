Welcome to Linguist

A Corvid-AI Research Project

Currently, only 13 languages of the estimated 1500 local African languages in Africa are supported by Google translate. This is mainly due to the lack of reliable, well documented and insufficient annotated datasets on local African languages.

Project Nightingale is an open source project aimed at providing a platform for gathering, documenting and annotating textual data on local African languages.

Please contribute to make this a success.
